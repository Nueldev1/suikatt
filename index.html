About Us

The SUIKATT Token project aims to create the largest utility backed meme-themed community on the SUI blockchain. The project will provide various benefits to its users, such as freemint dual-life NFTs, OG and early supporter NFTs, and public mint NFTs. This NFT will be the core components of the ecosystem. Additionally, the project will reward holders of its NFTs and the early SUI community members through a unique tokenomics structure while developing several utilities that will be key to growing the SUI ecosystem immediately after mainnet launch.
